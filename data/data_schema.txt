AI OPERATIONS ASSISTANT - DATA SCHEMA
=====================================

PROJECT PURPOSE:
E-commerce inventory and product management system for natural language queries.
Example queries: "How many Fire TV Sticks in stock?", "What's the highest-rated Electronics product?"

=====================================
FIELD DESCRIPTIONS
=====================================

PRODUCT IDENTIFICATION
----------------------
asin: Amazon Standard Identification Number (unique product ID)
product_name: Full product name/title
product_link: URL to Amazon product page
category: Product category (Electronics, Books, etc.)
rank: Bestseller rank within category

PRICING & RATINGS
-----------------
price: Product price in USD (cleaned, numeric)
rating: Customer rating (0-5 scale)
review_count: Total number of customer reviews
is_prime: Prime eligibility (Yes/No)

INVENTORY MANAGEMENT
--------------------
stock_quantity: Current units in stock (0-500)
warehouse_location: Storage location (Melbourne, Sydney, Brisbane, Perth)
restock_date: Scheduled restock date
supplier_lead_time_days: Days required for supplier delivery (3-14)
minimum_stock_threshold: Minimum stock level before restock alert (10-50)
last_sold_date: Most recent sale date
daily_sales_avg: Average daily sales volume (1-50)

CUSTOMER SATISFACTION METRICS
------------------------------
sentiment_score: Rating converted to 100-point scale (rating × 20)
satisfaction_level: Categorical rating (Excellent/Good/Average/Poor)
recommendation_score: Percentage recommendation score ((rating/5) × 100)
complaint_risk: Risk level based on rating (High if <4.0, else Low)

AI-READY FIELDS
---------------
product_description: Natural language summary of product details
similar_price_alternatives: Pipe-separated ASINs of similar-priced products (within 30%)
higher_rated_alternatives: Pipe-separated ASINs of higher-rated alternatives

METADATA
--------
scraped_at: Data collection timestamp
